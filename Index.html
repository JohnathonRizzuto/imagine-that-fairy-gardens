🚀 Setup Guide
Welcome to Freedom Financial CRM 🎉
This guide walks you through everything you need to get your CRM fully set up — from creating your Calendly booking link to connecting your Google Calendar. Follow these steps in order and you'll be live in under 30 minutes.
1
Set Up Your Profile
Your profile powers the entire CRM — your name appears on every text you send, your booking link is used on every client row, and your contact card is tied to your info. Do this first.
WHAT TO FILL IN
✅ First & Last Name — shows on texts, portals, and your contact card
✅ NPN Number — your National Producer Number (find it at nipr.com)
✅ Direct Phone — the number clients call or text you back on
✅ Business Email — appears on client portals
✅ Licensed States — e.g. IL, MS
✅ Calendly Link — see Step 2 below
✅ Google Calendar Embed — see Step 3 below
2
Create Your Calendly Booking Link
Sign Up Free →
Calendly lets clients book a call with you without the back-and-forth texting. You set your availability once, and they pick a time. It's free and takes 5 minutes to set up.
HOW TO SET IT UP — STEP BY STEP
1
Go to calendly.com/signup and create a free account using your business email.
2
Connect your Google Calendar when prompted — this lets Calendly check your availability automatically so clients never book when you're busy.
3
Click "New Event Type" → choose "One-on-One" → set the duration to 30 minutes → name it something like "Free Insurance Consultation".
4
Set your available hours — for example: Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm Central. Make sure your timezone is set to America/Chicago.
5
Click "Save & Close". You'll see your event. Click "Copy Link". Your link will look like:
https://calendly.com/yourname/30min
6
Paste that link into your CRM profile → Settings & Profile → Calendly Link → Save. Now every client row has a 📅 Book button that texts that link instantly.
💡 Pro tip: In Calendly, go to Integrations → Google Calendar and make sure it's connected. When a client books through your Calendly link, the appointment automatically appears in your Google Calendar and in your CRM's Booking & Calendar tab.
3
Embed Your Google Calendar in the CRM
Open Calendar →
This puts your live Google Calendar right inside the Booking & Calendar tab of your CRM. When clients book through Calendly, appointments show up here automatically.
HOW TO GET YOUR EMBED CODE
1
Go to calendar.google.com and make sure you're signed into the Google account you use for work.
2
On the left side, find "Other calendars" — click the + icon → "Create new calendar" → name it something like "Freedom Financial Appointments" → click Create calendar.
3
Click the three dots (⋮) next to your new calendar → click "Settings and sharing".
4
Scroll down to "Integrate calendar". You'll see an Embed code — it looks like this:
<iframe src="https://calendar.google.com/calendar/embed?src=abc123%40group.calendar.google.com&ctz=America%2FChicago" style="border: 0" width="800" height="600"></iframe>
5
Copy the entire iframe code → go to Settings & Profile in your CRM → paste it in the "Google Calendar Embed Code" field → Save.
6
Go to Booking & Calendar in the CRM — your live calendar now shows there. When Calendly is also connected to this same Google Calendar, every client booking appears here automatically.
💡 Connect Calendly to this calendar: In Calendly → Integrations → Google Calendar → make sure the calendar you just created ("Freedom Financial Appointments") is selected as the calendar where new bookings are added. That way everything syncs automatically.
4
How to Use the CRM — Every Tab Explained
🏠 Dashboard
Your home base. See total clients, leads, follow-ups, and active clients at a glance. Quick action buttons let you add a client, share your booking link, or import a CSV list.
👥 Clients
Your full client database. Add clients manually or import a CSV. Each client row has buttons to text them 💬, send a booking link 📅, send your contact card 📇, mark as Sold 🎉, edit ✏️, or delete.
📊 Pipeline
Paste leads directly from Google Sheets. Create separate lead sheets for different campaigns (IUL Truckers, Final Expense, etc.). Track each lead through stages: Interested → Contacted → Quote Sent → Application.
📅 Booking & Calendar
Your live Google Calendar lives here. Copy your Calendly link to share with clients. Switch between Week, Month, and Day views. Appointments booked through Calendly appear here automatically.
📚 Book of Business
Every client you've sold goes here. Track carrier, product, premium, draft date, app status, and payout. Stats show total sold, this month, this week, and approved. Draft day reminders pop up on the dashboard automatically.
📄 Client Portal
When you mark a client as Sold, their policy portal is auto-generated here. Click 📤 Send → downloads the portal + opens Messages with their number pre-filled. Attach the file and send — they get a full policy summary.
📇 My Contact Card
Your digital contact card. Tap 📲 Save to Contacts to save your own card. Use the QR code in person. Every client row has a 📇 button that texts them your contact link so they can save you in one tap.
⚙️ Settings & Profile
Edit all your info — name, NPN, phone, email, states, Calendly link, Google Calendar embed, and contact card settings. Changes update everywhere in the CRM instantly. Use Danger Zone to reset or clear data.
5
Your Daily Workflow
🌅
Morning — Check Dashboard
Look for draft day reminders (clients whose policy drafts tomorrow). Call or text them to confirm their account is funded. Check follow-ups and hot leads.
📞
Working Leads — Pipeline Tab
Paste new leads from Google Sheets into a lead sheet. Use the 📅 Book button to text each lead your booking link. Move them through stages as you make contact.
🎉
After a Sale — Sold Flow
Click 🎉 Sold on the client → fill in carrier, policy type, premium, draft date → Save. Their portal is auto-generated. Go to Client Portal tab → click 📤 Send → attach the file in Messages → send. Done.
📇
New Client — Send Your Contact
After every call, hit the 📇 button on that client's row → Messages opens with their number and your contact link pre-filled → send. They tap the link → save your contact. Now they always have your number.
✅
You're all set!
Once your profile is saved, Calendly is connected, and your calendar is embedded — your CRM is fully live. Start adding clients and closing deals.
